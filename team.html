About the Program

Singh Capital Partners offers a rigorous, hands-on internship for exceptional students who want exposure to private markets investing across venture capital, private equity, and real assets.

Interns work directly alongside the investment team — sourcing deals, conducting due diligence, building financial models, and contributing to portfolio management. This is not a passive learning experience.

We recruit from top universities and value intellectual curiosity, analytical rigor, and a genuine passion for building.

Investment Research
Source and evaluate investment opportunities, build financial models, and prepare investment memos for the senior team.
Portfolio Operations
Work directly with portfolio companies on strategic initiatives, market analysis, and operational improvements.
Government Affairs
Support our policy and advocacy work, tracking regulatory developments relevant to our investment thesis.
