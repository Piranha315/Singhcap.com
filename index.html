Our Approach

Disciplined.
Strategic. Long-Term.

We combine institutional rigor with entrepreneurial agility, investing across private markets to build companies, assets, and communities that endure.

Venture & Growth
+

Partnering with visionary founders in technology, healthcare, and industrial innovation — combining strategic insight and operational support to scale transformative companies.

Private Equity & Roll-Ups
+

Acquiring and scaling proven businesses through buy-and-build strategies, unlocking operational efficiency and compounding enterprise value.

Real Assets
+

Targeting resilient commercial and mixed-use properties in growth markets, emphasizing strategic location, strong cash flow, and responsible stewardship.

Funds & Co-Investments
+

Investing alongside top-tier fund managers globally, with emphasis on governance, alignment, and consistent long-term performance.

Civic Engagement
+

Guided by Sikh principles of Seva, supporting education, inclusion, and policy initiatives that align purpose with prosperity.

Government Affairs
+

Engaging in strategic policy advocacy and partnerships to shape impactful legislation and foster civic progress.
