Philanthropy & Foundation

Our Mission

Singh Capital Philanthropies is the philanthropic arm of our Singh Capital office, dedicated to uplifting communities, expanding opportunity, and strengthening the institutions that build a thriving America. Guided by Sikh values of seva (selfless service), leadership, and integrity, we support initiatives that advance the Sikh community, empower America's citizens and military, expand access to tennis worldwide, and cultivate the next generation of leaders.
