Philanthropy & Foundation

Our Mission

Singh Capital Philanthropies is the philanthropic arm of our Singh Capital office, dedicated to uplifting communities, expanding opportunity, and strengthening the institutions that build a thriving America. Guided by Sikh values of seva (selfless service), leadership, and integrity, we support initiatives that advance the Sikh community, empower America's citizens and military, expand access to tennis worldwide, and cultivate the next generation of leaders.

Philanthropic Contributions

A values-driven approach to advancing meaningful impact

  • The leading pediatric health system in the Washington, D.C. area.

  • An online giving platform rooted in the Sikh spirit of sharing one's earnings for the betterment of society.

  • Representing Sikhs and Sikh institutions to governmental and non-governmental entities worldwide.

  • Expanding access to medical devices in developing countries for an equitable standard of women's care.

  • Providing humanitarian aid worldwide, regardless of race, religion, faith, or nationality.

  • Developing young people's character through tennis and education for a lifetime of success.

  • The nation's oldest Sikh organization, building leadership in the Sikh American community.

  • Delivering warm vegetarian meals to students at Title I schools across the DMV area.

  • Advancing Sikh American rights through advocacy in communities, classrooms, and Congress.

  • Connecting people with the teachings of Sikhi and strengthening bonds across the global Sikh community.

  • Collecting and exhibiting artifacts of historical significance related to U.S. Space Force heritage.
