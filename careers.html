Singh Capital Partners  ·  We're hiring

We don't just hire
for the role. We hire leaders.

Singh Capital Partners is a family office backing exceptional founders building transformative companies. We invest across direct opportunities, funds, and co-investments in RE, PE, and VC — sitting at the nexus of the tech, financial, and political hubs of the country. Beyond our investment platform, we are building out Singh PAC and Singh Capital Philanthropies, giving our team rare and meaningful exposure across capital, policy, and impact at scale. It's an exciting time to join us.

We are deliberately assembling a team of sharp, experienced professionals who know how to source, evaluate, and execute — and who bring something more to the table. The best candidates will have real investment chops, strong analytical foundations, and the drive to operate at the highest level. What sets our team apart is that we also seek out people who have been deeply excellent at something outside of work: D1 and professional athletes, musicians, artists, builders, and those who have gone all-in on a pursuit that most wouldn't. We value self-starters, natural connectors, multilingual and multicultural thinkers, and people invested in mentoring the next generation. Credentials matter — so does character.

We are currently hiring across Senior Leadership, Investment Team, Communications, Operations, and unique but crucial utility player roles. If any of these sound like you, we'd love to connect — apply and come see us in our offices across Washington DC, New York City, and San Francisco.

The pitch

"We want the athlete who still trains like they're competing. The musician who hears structure where others hear noise. The artist who sees what isn't there yet. The connector who makes every room better. The self-starter who doesn't wait to be told."

What makes someone great at investing — really great — rarely comes from a textbook. It comes from having been deeply, relentlessly passionate about something hard. The discipline of a D1 athlete. The ear of a trained musician. The eye of a working artist. The instinct of someone who's built something from nothing.

We deliberately seek out people whose lives have been shaped by excellence outside the ordinary — because that's exactly the kind of judgment, grit, and creativity this work demands.

Who we're looking for

Proven excellence
D1 or professional athletes, national competitors, performers — people who know what reaching the top of something truly hard feels like.
Unique, rare skills
Musicians, artists, builders, makers. People who went deep on something most wouldn't. That depth of passion translates directly into how we think and invest.
Natural connectors
People who light up a room and bring others together — across languages, cultures, and backgrounds. We are a global firm and our team should reflect that.
Self-starters
You see what needs doing and you do it. You're energized by ownership, move with urgency, and never wait for permission to lead.
Multilingual & multicultural
We value team members who speak multiple languages and carry the cultural intelligence to build trust across the world's most dynamic markets.
Mentors & multipliers
We build careers here, not just fill seats. We want people who are as invested in lifting up the next generation as they are in their own growth.

Experience levels range from Analyst to VP across RE, PE, and VC. We care far less about where you've been and far more about what you've built, how you think, and who you are.

What we're building

This isn't one platform — it's three, converging at the most consequential intersection in the country: tech, finance, and politics.

Every person we hire touches all three. The scope is intentional — we want generalists with depth, not specialists with blinders.

Our culture

We are a small, focused, deeply ambitious team — and we intend to stay that way. We work hard together and we play hard together. The best conversations happen after hours. The best ideas come from unexpected places.

We don't believe in hierarchy for hierarchy's sake. Everyone is here to lead — in their role, on their team, and in the broader ecosystem we're helping to build. If you want a place where your background, your passions, and your full self are genuinely valued, this is it.
